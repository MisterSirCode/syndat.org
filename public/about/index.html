Goal and Plans

Ultimately, syndat is, or will one day be, a compilation of material science information regarding exotic materials and synthetic minerals. Additionally, a future goal is to include even non-crystalline materials from all walks of science.

I have always had an interest in Artificial Materials, but as a common joe with little networking, it became difficult to source or learn about the materials I adore. This has been a major part of my motivation in this project. I started development back on May 28th, 2024, and Ive been working on it off and on since. I hope over time I can let this project grow and expand to where it can be a useful reference and tool for many people.

I was inspired to create Syndat based off of Mindat and Gemdat. (We are unaffiliated)
Feel free to give them a visit over at Mindat.org and Gemdat.org
