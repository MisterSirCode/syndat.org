Goal and Plans

Ultimately, syndat is, or will one day be, a compilation of material science information regarding exotic materials and synthetic minerals.

I have always had an interest in Artificial Materials, but as a common joe with little networking, it became difficult to source or learn about the materials I adore. This has been a major part of my motivation in this project. I hope over time I can let this project grow and expand to where I want it.
